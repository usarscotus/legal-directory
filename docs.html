SCOTUS Document Library

This library provides access to core Supreme Court templates, including petitions, briefs, and appendix formats. All documents are hosted on Google Docs and open in a new tab.

These templates are maintained by the USAR Supreme Court to ensure consistency in filings across all proceedings. Check back periodically for updated or additional materials.
